About

The Father’s Playbook was developed specifically for guys in response to the lack of information for fathers as they navigate their way to fatherhood. We are grateful to the fathers who have participated in the research that continues to support and inform the app and to those who have consulted with us through several development cycles.

The Father’s Playbook is a collaboration between:

  • The Safe Babies team at UTHealth Houston School of Public Health led by Dr. Dorothy Mandell,
  • Center for Health Communication at UT Austin led by Dr. Micheal Mackert,
  • and SAGA Lab at UT Austin led by Dr. Paul Toprac

We are also lucky to have a team of staff and students who have worked to develop and bring the app to fathers.
