About

The Father’s Playbook was developed at the Center for Health Communication at The University of Texas at Austin in response to the lack of information help for guys – designed especially for guys – whose partners are expecting a baby.

After the foundational development work on Father’s Playbook, in came the Texas Safe Babies team at University of Texas Health Science Center Tyler and then the SAGA team at UT Austin to help turn this work into a fully functioning app.

In addition to our design and app development wizards, The Father’s Playbook Team has experts in health communication, maternal and child health, and infant development.
